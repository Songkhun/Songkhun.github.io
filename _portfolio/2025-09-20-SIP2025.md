I receiving a research grant from the Ministry of Science and ICT of the Republic of Korea to participate in the Scientist Invitation Program to Korea 2025 (SIP 2025), organized by INITHiNK, a research and development (R&D) consulting company.
The program took place from July 1 to September 26, 2025, in Seoul and Daejeon, Republic of Korea, and brought together 35 invited scientists from five APEC member economies: Thailand, Indonesia, Malaysia, Peru, and Vietnam. It aims to promote knowledge exchange, foster collaborative research, and expand international academic networks.

During the program, I collaborated with Dr. Seong Kon Lee, Principal Investigator at the Korea Institute of Geoscience and Mineral Resources (KIGAM), conducting research on machine learning for soil classification and geotechnical data analysis.
